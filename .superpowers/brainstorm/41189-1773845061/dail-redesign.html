Dail.ai — Full App Redesign

Making the value obvious. Every screen must answer: "Why should I open this instead of ChatGPT?"

PROBLEM Why users don't see the value today

Looks like another notes app

The home screen shows tasks + memories but doesn't demonstrate the AI intelligence. Users think "this is just Todoist with a different font."

Meeting recording feels hidden

The #1 wow feature — bot-free meeting recording — is buried in a small button. Should be front-and-center, impossible to miss.

No "aha moment" in first 30 seconds

The app opens to sample data. There's no guided first experience that makes users go "oh wow, this is different."

Streaks feel like homework

The streak is a number in a corner. No story, no motivation, no "why should I care?" The reflection feels optional, not essential.

REDESIGN The 5 Pillars

1

Meeting Intelligence — The Hero Feature

Genspark-level

Meeting recording becomes the centerpiece, not a side feature. One tap from anywhere — widget, home, calendar. The summary output is best-in-class.

Big Record Button on Home

Floating red ⏺ button always visible. Tap → recording starts in 500ms. No consent screen (already approved). System notification shows timer.

Genspark-style Meeting Summary

After recording: TL;DR (2 sentences), Key Decisions (bullet), Action Items (with owners + due dates auto-linked to tasks), Open Questions, Full Transcript with speaker labels. Shareable as PDF.

Pre-Meeting Brief

5 min before meeting: notification with context from last meeting with same title/attendees. "Last time you discussed X. Action item Y is still open."

Meeting Analytics

Talk-time ratio, meeting duration trends, action item completion rate. "You've had 12h of meetings this week — 3h more than average."

Whisper large-v3 Claude Sonnet Speaker diarization
2

Smart Todo — Voice-First Task Management

Differentiator

Todos aren't just checkboxes. They're voice-recordable, AI-enriched, context-aware items that know where they came from.

Voice Todo Recording

Hold mic button on any task → record voice note attached to that todo. "Let me explain what I mean by this task..." Transcribed and searchable.

AI Task Enrichment

"Send proposal" → AI adds context: "Related to Meijer BTEQ migration. Last discussed March 15 in ART call. Spot instance config doc due Thursday."

Work/Personal Toggle

Clear visual separation. Work = teal theme, Professional context, meeting-linked tasks. Personal = purple theme, life tasks, habits, reminders.

Natural Language Input

"Add send proposal to work tasks due Thursday" → creates task, tags work, sets due date. "Remind me to call mom at 7pm" → task + reminder.

3

Calendar Intelligence — Not Just a Timeline

Must be strong

The calendar isn't a view of events — it's a command center that shows what happened, what's coming, and what needs attention.

Day View with Meeting Notes Inline

Each meeting event shows: status (recorded/upcoming/missed), quick summary preview, action item count. Tap to expand full notes without leaving calendar.

AI Focus Blocks

Dail auto-detects your peak focus hours and suggests blocking them. Shows "2h deep work available" or "Today is fully booked — 6.5h meetings."

Week Heat Map

Color-coded week strip: green (light day), yellow (balanced), red (overloaded). At a glance know which days need protection.

Tasks on Timeline

Tasks with due times appear on the calendar timeline alongside meetings. See your full day — meetings + tasks + focus blocks — in one view.

4

Streaks & Purpose — Why You Keep Coming Back

Retention engine

Streaks aren't a number — they're a story about who you are becoming. The reflection becomes the most meaningful 30 seconds of your day.

Streak Story on Home

Not just "🔥 7" — show "7 days of showing up. Your longest streak: 15. Last break: 3 weeks ago." Make it personal, make it a narrative.

Weekly Wins Summary

Every Sunday: "This week: 42 tasks completed, 6 meetings recorded, 5 reflections. Your most productive day: Wednesday." AI-generated celebration.

Streak Milestones

7 days → "Week warrior" badge. 30 days → "Monthly master". 100 days → special animation. Share-worthy achievements that feel earned.

Evening Reflection Redesign

Show the AI-generated day summary FIRST (wow factor), then mood picker, then optional journal. End with: "Streak extended! See you tomorrow at 7:30am."

5

AI Wow Moments — Make Intelligence Visible

Differentiation

Every screen should have at least one moment where the user thinks "how did it know that?"

Proactive Morning Brief

"Today you have 3 meetings. The Meijer call has an open action item from last time. Your busiest block is 1-4pm."

Context-Rich Reminders

When "Plan Paris" reminder fires: attached is the flight research, hotel options, and your March 12 note about wanting to visit Le Marais.

Cross-Memory Connections

"You mentioned headaches 3 times this week in reflections. Last time this happened was during the Meijer project crunch."

Smart Capture Classification

Type naturally → AI instantly classifies as task/reminder/note/question. Shows visual confirmation: "✓ Task created" with the right tags auto-applied.

Meeting Prep Nudge

5 min before each meeting: push notification with last meeting's context, open action items, and key decisions to reference.

Weekly Pattern Insights

"You're most productive 9-11am. Your mood dips on Mondays. You complete 80% of tasks when set with due dates."
