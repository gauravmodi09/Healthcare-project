## Description (4000 chars)

Never miss a dose again. MedCare is the intelligent medicine tracker built for Indian families — turning messy prescriptions into organised care plans with smart reminders that actually work.

**SCAN PRESCRIPTIONS IN SECONDS**
Photograph your doctor's prescription — even handwritten ones. MedCare's AI reads the prescription and extracts every medicine name, dosage, frequency, and duration automatically. For extra accuracy, snap your medicine strips too. Our dual-capture AI cross-references both sources so nothing gets missed. Review everything before confirming — you're always in control.

**REMINDERS THAT FIT YOUR LIFE**
Not all reminders are equal. MedCare understands "before breakfast" and "after dinner" — adapting to your actual routine, not arbitrary clock times. Smart snooze learns your habits. Dynamic Island keeps your next dose visible without opening the app. Critical medicines like blood thinners get extra-persistent alerts. You'll never wonder "did I take that tablet?" again.

**YOUR FAMILY, ONE APP**
Managing medicines for ageing parents? Tracking your child's antibiotics course? MedCare supports up to 5 family profiles from a single account. Each member gets their own schedule, their own reminders, their own adherence history. Check on Papa's BP medicines from across the city. Get notified if Amma misses her Thyronorm.

**AI HEALTH COMPANION**
Have a health question at 11 PM? MedCare's AI chat understands your active medicines and gives contextual answers. Ask about side effects, drug interactions, or symptoms. Get structured responses with actionable advice and clear guidance on when to see a doctor. Not a replacement for medical consultation — but a reliable first point of reference.

**TRACK WHAT MATTERS**
Visual adherence charts show exactly who took what and when. Daily, weekly, and monthly views help you spot patterns. Celebrating a 30-day streak of perfect adherence? MedCare notices. Need to show your doctor your medication history? Export a clean PDF report in one tap.

**BUILT FOR INDIA**
Indian prescriptions are unique — handwritten, multilingual, using abbreviations like "BD" and "TDS" that generic apps don't understand. MedCare is trained on thousands of Indian prescriptions from across specialties. We understand Indian medicine brands, packaging formats, and the way doctors here actually write. This isn't a foreign app localised for India — it's built here, for you.

**PRIVACY FIRST**
Your health data stays on your device. We use industry-standard encryption for anything that syncs. No data selling, no ads, no compromise. Your prescriptions are yours.

Start with our free plan — upgrade to Pro or Premium when your family needs more.

Download MedCare today. Your family's health deserves better than sticky notes and memory.
