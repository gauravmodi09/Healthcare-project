## Description
MedCare is the all-in-one health companion designed for Indian families. Simply scan any prescription with your camera, and our AI instantly extracts every medicine — name, dosage, schedule, and duration — turning messy handwriting into smart, timely reminders.

KEY FEATURES:

AI Prescription Scanner
Point your camera at any prescription. MedCare reads doctor handwriting, identifies medicines from India's largest drug database, and sets up your complete schedule in seconds.

Smart Medicine Reminders
Never miss a dose again. Get timely notifications with medicine name, dosage, and instructions. Track adherence with beautiful visual reports. Live Activities keep your current dose visible on your Lock Screen.

Family Health Profiles
Manage medicines for your entire family — parents, kids, and grandparents — all from one app. Each profile tracks its own medications, vitals, and health records independently.

Doctor Connect
Book appointments with verified doctors, receive e-prescriptions directly in the app, and access teleconsultation when you need quick advice. Doctors get a dedicated dashboard to manage patients remotely.

Health Records Vault
Store prescriptions, lab reports, and medical documents securely. Organize by family member and share instantly with any doctor during visits.

Vitals Tracking
Log blood pressure, blood sugar, weight, temperature, and SpO2. Visualize trends over time and get AI-powered insights about your health patterns.

AI Health Chat
Ask health questions in English or Hindi and get reliable, medically-reviewed answers. Understand side effects, drug interactions, and medication guidelines anytime.

ABHA Integration
Connect your Ayushman Bharat Health Account for seamless digital health record management across India's healthcare ecosystem.

Elder Mode
Large text, simplified navigation, and high-contrast design — purpose-built for senior family members who need medication management most.

Built for India. Private by design. Your health data stays on your device.
