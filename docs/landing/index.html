The Problem

Managing medicines shouldn't be this hard

Every day, millions of Indians struggle with confusing prescriptions and forgotten doses. The consequences are real.

Forgetting doses

Between work calls and school runs, that afternoon Metformin slips through. Missed doses mean slower recovery and wasted money.

57% of Indians miss doses regularly

Scattered prescriptions

Paper prescriptions pile up. When your doctor asks "what are you taking?" you're stuck guessing. Critical dosage details lost in illegible handwriting.

3.2 avg active prescriptions per family

No way to track progress

Are the medicines working? Is your father actually taking his BP tablets? Without tracking, families are left anxious and doctors decide with incomplete data.

68% of doctors want adherence data
